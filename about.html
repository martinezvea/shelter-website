    General adoption

    Cats and dogs are for adoption at East Village Animal Shelter.

    Fees are:

    * $175 per cat

    * $200 per kitten or $300 per pair

    * $295 per dog (includes collar and leash)

    * Puppies under the age of 1 year are $395 (includes collar and leash)

    Fee covers spay/neuter, vaccines, and microchip.

    Many adoptions can be done same-day. Others may take more time to complete.

    You may print and fill out the Adoption Application ahead of time, but we will only accept applications in person. Please DO NOT MAIL OR FAX it in.


    Animal Intake

    East Village Animal Shelter does accept owner relinquishments from public. Cats and dogs will be given a thorough behavior and medical evaluation prior to admission.

    EVAS does not accept animals with a history of aggression or biting.

    Policy

    East Village Animal Shelter has a Conflict of Interest Policy
    Board members and staff notify all board members of any perceived or potential conflicts of interest and abstain from voting on any issues where a conflict of interest has been determined to exist.

    East Village Animal Shelter has a Whistleblower Policy
    If any employee reasonably believes that some policy, practice, or activity of East Village Animal Shelter is in violation of law, a written complaint must be filed by that employee with the Executive Director or the Board President. It is the intent of East Village Animal Shelter to adhere to all laws and regulations that apply to the organization and the underlying purpose of this policy is to support the organization’s goal of legal compliance. The support of all employees is necessary to achieving compliance with various laws and regulations. An employee is protected from retaliation only if the employee brings the alleged unlawful activity, policy, or practice to the attention of East Village Animal Shelter and provides the East Village Animal Shelter with a reasonable opportunity to investigate and correct the alleged unlawful activity. The protection described below is only available to employees that comply with this requirement. East Village Animal Shelter will not retaliate against an employee who in good faith, has made a protest or raised a complaint against some practice of East Village Animal Shelter, or of another individual or entity with whom East Village Animal Shelter has a business relationship, on the basis of a reasonable belief that the practice is in violation of law, or a clear mandate of public policy. East Village Animal Shelter will not retaliate against employees who disclose or threaten to disclose to a supervisor or a public body, any activity, policy, or practice of East Village Animal Shelter that the employee reasonably believes is in violation of a law, or a rule, or regulation mandated pursuant to law or is in violation of a clear mandate of public policy concerning.

    East Village Animal Shelter has an Executive Director Compensation Policy
    The salary of the Executive Director is determined by the Board of Directors. Any change in this salary must be presented and voted on by the Board. Decisions are only made by a majority vote.

    East Village Animal Shelter has a Donor Privacy Policy
    East Village Animal Shelter does not share, sell or rent donor information. Donor information is only used to communicate with donors via email, mail and phone. Donors are always offered the opportunity to remove their name from our list.
